## Feature list
- Skill XP/Level spooefr
- Slayer XP/Level spoofer
- Coin/Bit/Any Currency spoofer
- Rank changer
- IGN spoofer
- Lobby ID spoofer
- Appearance editor — click the **paintbrush** in your inventory to open it, with a live player preview:
  - Armor — click a slot, pick a color on a real color wheel + an armor trim (pattern & material)
  - Cape — the official Mojang capes (MineCon, Migrator, …) or a custom cape from a PNG file/URL
  - Head — wear any of ~4900 Hypixel SkyBlock heads (searchable icons) or your own texture
- many more to come!

> All appearance spoofing (cape / armor / head) is **client-side self-view only** — it changes
> what you see on your own screen, not what other players see. Now targeting Minecraft 26.1.2.
